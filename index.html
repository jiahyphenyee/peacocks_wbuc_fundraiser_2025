Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How does this fundraiser work?
Donors can select from 2 types of baseline runs.

For $2, 1 baseline run will be added to the team's total. These runs will be distributed evenly among the team.
For $5, you can assign 1 baseline run to a specific player on the team.
How do I contribute?
1. Click on support button above to start.
2. Enter the donation amount / no. of baseline runs
3. You will be redirected to a form to transfer funds and upload payment proof.
How long will this fundraiser go on?
It will be open to contributions till the end of September 2025.
More examples
For $11, you can assign 3 runs to the team & 1 run to Deme,

(3 x $2) + (1 x $5) = $11

For $50, you can assign 8 runs to Yanling & 2 runs to Prem

(8 + 2) x $5 = $50
